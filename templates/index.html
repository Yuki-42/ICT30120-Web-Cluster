Who We Are

We are a software development and standards company that specialises in digital communications. We develop the popular open-source Echo protocol, which is used by dozens of people worldwide. Our mission is to raise the bar for communication software and allow total user freedom in how they communicate. We believe that communication is a fundamental human right and should be protected at all costs.

Example image

What we do

We develop the Echo protocol, a secure and open-source communication protocol that allows users to communicate with each other without fear of surveillance or censorship. We also develop the Echo client, a user-friendly application that allows users to easily communicate with each other using the Echo protocol. We also provide consulting services to companies that want to use the Echo protocol in their products. We help them integrate the protocol into their products and provide ongoing support to ensure that their products are secure and reliable. Have a look at our gallery to see some of the projects we have worked on.
